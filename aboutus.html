About Us

Market Scanner

  • Who are We

    Market Scanner was established by a group of four students from RMIT University. Our passion is to create a platform that integrates the data from all big online marketplaces to accelerate users' searching process in their e-commerce experience Apart from that innovative search engine, we also provide many other features in our applications with an aim to enhance customer experience with the e-commerce world.

    The e-commerce world has been evolving dramatically, while we realised that customers often find it difficult to search for their prefered products. Due to the great availability of products from numerous marketplaces, customers usually need to spend hours online just to find and purchase a single item. This is the area to which Market Scanner targets to bring users with better online-shopping experience.

  • Why are we different?

    What sets Market Scanner apart from our competitors in the market is that we are the first and only company in Australia that has exclusive partnership with big marketplace platforms like Gumtree, Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, etc. We are granted exclusive access to their catalogue databases to pull the data that is necessary to help accelerate and facilitate your searching process.

    The partnership we have with those marketplace platforms covers the vast majority of e-commerece industry in Australia, so there would be nothing that we do not have access to.
